Real Estate Market Analysis & Predictive Modeling

Business Challenge: Identify the most important drivers of real estate prices in California and build predictive tools for agents and investors.

Approach:

  • Analyzed housing data: median income, proximity to ocean, house characteristics, population density.
  • Built models: Linear Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost.
  • Performed feature importance analysis – identified income & location as top predictors.
  • Evaluated model accuracy using RMSE & R²; visualized predictions for validation.

Skills Used: Python, Machine Learning (XGBoost, RF, Linear Regression), Feature Engineering, Visualization

Results:

  • XGBoost achieved RMSE ≈ $47,215 and R² = 0.83
  • Coastal areas consistently had higher prices, even at lower incomes
  • Inland properties had better potential for affordable housing and future growth

Strategic Impact:

  • Real estate firms can adjust pricing strategies & build predictive valuation tools
  • Urban planners can use the model to plan affordable housing
  • Marketing teams can target campaigns by region and buyer type
